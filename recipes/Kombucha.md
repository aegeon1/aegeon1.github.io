2 Gallons (7.6L) of Kombucha:

- 16 cups of filtered water
- 24 grams of black tea (12 tea bags)
- 1.5 cups white sugar

Modified for 2.5L:

- 5 cups of filtered water
- 8 grams of black tea (4 tea bags)
- 0.5 cups white sugar
